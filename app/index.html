WELCOME TO XIRKA

Xirka Silicon Technology was established in May, 2008 as a fabless company focusing in WiMAX baseband chipset, we deliver a System-on-Chip (SoC) for Broadband Wireless Access (BWA) to fulfill the needs for conducting businesses, supporting everybody's dynamic lifestyle which very often requires immediate attention and action. To provide assurance, our SoC uses IEEE 802.16d-2004 standard for nomadic WiMAX and IEEE 802.16e-2005 for mobile WiMAX.

Continuing commitments as a design company, since 2011 we started to develop SIM card and proceed with the smart card products and derivatives such as access card, ID card, secured card for banking by using a technology called Near Field Communication and RFID.

There are many IT equipment that will benefit from our chipset, such as, modem cards, computers, notebooks, mobile gadgets, CPE modems, etc. Of course, we also understand the need of our clients to have a reliability chipset with affordable price, therefore we built our research and design center with those concerns in mind.

Xirka technology definitely is an innovative chipset for dynamic life.

Xirka are experienced in designing chip with the latest technologies. Our expertise range from system design and modeling to ASIC implementation. We also have strong relationships with leading chip fabrication and IP vendors that allow us to deliver a high quality chips; DSP/PHY System Modeling, SoC System Architecture Design, RTL Coding (VHDL/Verilog/System C), Front End and Back End ASIC Physical Design, FPGA Prototyping, MAC Layer Software Design, Wireless System Testing (RCT, PCT, IOT), Design for Test and System Verifcation.

We understand that technology behind the product is as important as the company itself. Our main assets is the technology, and we maintain this technology carefully. Here are some of our latest technologies and remember, the only thing more amazing than these technologies are with you do with them; Low Complexity & High Performance SM & Space Time Transmit Diversity MIMO System, Multi Cores and Multi Bus System on Chip Architecture, Advance Channel Coding Architecture : Turbo Coding, Reed Solomon, BCH and LDPC Based, High Mobility OFDM System Synchronizer and Equalizer

Throughout product milestones, Xirka have demonstrated the ability to produce cost efective chips without sacrifcing the quality. We have fexibility to adapt customer demand, and we applied efective management approach to ensure we met the delivery time; Customization, Full Service, Afordable Cost, Cost Reduction, Delivery Time, System Solution.
